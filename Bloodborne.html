Bloodborne was released in 2015, taking on a more Gothic style. With Bloodborne came the use of guns and "trick weapons." These weapons, instead of simply using two hands to wield them, would either transform to utilize different attack styles, or use a hidden ability that makes it stronger. Instead of having a type of drink, your character, owing to the game's name, takes blood injections to heal. A wide variety of items are said to be derived from blood, including bullets, gems, and upgrade materials.
